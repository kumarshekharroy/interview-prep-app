# Behavioral Bank

This bank contains behavioral and managerial interview prompts with expected answer points and acceptance criteria for senior engineering roles.

## STAR-R Format

Use this structure:

* Situation: context.
* Task: responsibility.
* Action: what you personally did.
* Result: measurable or concrete outcome.
* Reflection: what you learned or changed.

## Scoring Rubric

Score each answer out of 20.

| Area | Points | Expected Evidence |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Specific story | 4 | real situation, not generic claim |
| Personal action | 5 | clear ownership and decisions |
| Result | 4 | measurable or concrete outcome |
| Reflection | 3 | learning and maturity |
| Delivery | 4 | concise, positive, senior-level |

Passing score: 15.

## BEH-001 - Tell Me About Yourself

### Expected Answer Points

* 9 years full-stack experience.
* .NET/ASP.NET Core/EF Core backend strength.
* React/TypeScript frontend capability.
* Azure/cloud-native preparation.

### Acceptance Criteria

* Under 2 minutes.
* Clear target role.
* No long chronology.

## Behavioral Answer Checklist

Before using an answer, verify:

* It is true.
* It has a clear situation.
* Your action is specific.
* The result is concrete.
* It includes reflection.
* It is not bitter.
* It is under 3 minutes.

## Weak Answer Fixes

| Weakness | Fix |
| --- | --- |
| too vague | add concrete context and action |
| too long | cut setup and focus on action/result |
| no result | add measurable or qualitative outcome |
| sounds negative | reframe around learning and ownership |
| no reflection | add what changed after the story |
