# Recruiter Outreach Templates

This file contains recruiter, referral, and follow-up templates for senior full-stack, senior .NET backend, Azure-focused, and lead engineer roles.

## Outreach Rules

1. Keep first messages short.
2. Mention target role and relevant stack.
3. Include one clear ask.
4. Personalize when possible.
5. Track every message.
6. Follow up once after about 5 business days.
7. Do not over-message the same person.

## Recruiter Screen Questions To Ask

Ask these during recruiter calls:

1. What team is this role for?
2. Is the role backend-heavy, full-stack, or frontend-heavy?
3. What is the primary stack?
4. How much Azure/cloud ownership is expected?
5. What is the interview process?
6. What level is the role mapped to internally?
7. What is the compensation range for the role?
8. Is the role remote, hybrid, or onsite?
9. What is the expected notice period/start date?
10. What are the biggest success criteria for the first 6 months?

## Outreach Cadence

| Day | Action |
| --- | --- |
| Day 0 | send first message |
| Day 5 business days | send one follow-up |
| Day 10-14 | mark inactive if no response |
| Any response | update tracker same day |

## Message Quality Checklist

Before sending:

* Is the role target clear?
* Is the stack fit visible?
* Is the ask specific?
* Is the message under 120 words when possible?
* Is the company/role personalized?
* Is the tone respectful and calm?
