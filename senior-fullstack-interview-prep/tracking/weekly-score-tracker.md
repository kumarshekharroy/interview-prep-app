# Weekly Score Tracker

Use this file to track progress across all 24 weeks. Scores are evidence-based, not mood-based.

## Scoring Rules

Each week has a score out of 100.

| Score | Meaning | Action |
| ---: | --- | --- |
| 85-100 | strong | continue and maintain |
| 75-84 | passing | continue with light revision |
| 60-74 | partial | complete targeted recovery within 48 hours |
| below 60 | blocked readiness | complete recovery before advancing if topic is foundational |
